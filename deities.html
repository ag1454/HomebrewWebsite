Deities

Ha’almehen

Ha’almehen is an ancient water elemental from the Plane of Water. Though it primarily resides there, it is not uncommon for it to cross into the Material Plane and watch over its waters. Centuries of its visitations have resulted in it being regarded as a sea god known as Sedsed, though it possesses no clerics. It also has no warlocks. Ha’almehen is very secretive and rarely makes appearances to mortals; if it does appear, it is reported it appears as a leviathan. Ha’almehen is part of a group of elementals who wish to prevent The Bridge from ravaging the Material Plane and undoing its natural balance.

Alignment: Neutral
Symbol: a tidal wave

Ilican

An alien entity from the Far Realm also known as The Bridge, it presents itself as an angelic and caring being on the Material Plane. In reality, Ilican is destructive, enjoys driving its followers to commit heinous deeds in the name of “worship,” and desires to have as many realities as it can under its influence.

Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Cleric Domains: Bridge
Warlock Patron Archetypes: Great Old One
Symbol: a ball of light holding a bridge-like structure

Irune

Worshiped by nearly all people of Irunia for thousands of years, she is regarded as the embodiment of all things good and viewed as a protector goddess. Irune sealed Vahagn away thousands of years ago, but was split into three separate beings when it resurfaced.

Alignment: Lawful Good
Cleric Domains: Peace, Light
Symbol: a jasmine flower holding a sphere of opal

Mercy

Also known as The Overseer, Mercy is a mysterious deity with unknown origins. His doctrine centers on blood’s power as a natural life force within all who possess it, the balance of life and death, and sacrifice. He developed the magic of hemocraft. It is said he once created a whole universe from a single drop of his own blood. Those who worship him are not deterred by his aloof and cryptic nature. It is not uncommon for vampires to have their weddings officiated under a priest or cleric of Mercy.

Alignment: Lawful Neutral
Cleric Domains: Blood
Symbol: a palm that is sliced and bleeding

Mauknave

Mauknave is a hedonistic being who lost his position as a god due to his less than desirable behavior, but still retains many of his godlike powers. He harbors souls in his body, a terrifying creation that resembles a jabberwock. His selfish nature has diminished greatly since he has been reunited with his lover, a saint of his brother-god. The stories that were initially told of a raging god quelled by a revived saint turned into the stories of a love between a reformed monster and the only one who was not frightened by him.

Alignment: Chaotic Evil
Cleric Domains: Souls
Warlock Pact Archetypes: Fiend
Symbol: the vague shape of a torso dripping with a black, inky substance

Nivensolem

A tender and merciful god who primarily takes the form of a growing tree that resembles a human man, symbolic of his position as the god who bestows life upon all creatures. Nivensolem follows the rules of his fellow gods, but is charismatic enough to sway their opinions in his favor if necessary. He desires nothing but the best for his followers, deeply devoted to every one of them. He is “brother” to the god of souls, Mauknave.

Alignment: Lawful Good
Cleric Domains: Life
Symbol: a hand coiled with vines with a sapling sprouting from the palm

Vahagn

An ancient and chaotic force, Irune sealed it thousands of years ago after it attempted to consume the land and its people. Vahagn was able to resurface and split Irune into three beings. However, the three beings had enough combined power to confine Vahagn in Irune’s royal castle.

Alignment: Chaotic Evil
Warlock Pact Archetypes: Fiend
Symbol:: a bestial eye with ram’s horns on either side
