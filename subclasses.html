Subclasses

Bridge Domain (Cleric)

Clerics of this domain serve Ilican, The Bridge. They respect and fear the powers beyond this plane of reality and seek to introduce them to the Material Plane.

Bridge Domain Spells:

  • 1st - Dissonant Whispers, Cause Fear
  • 3rd - Slow, Blink
  • 5th - Contact Other Plane, Dominate Person
  • 7th - Reverse Gravity, Forcecage
  • 9th - Weird, Time Stop

Ilican’s Knowledge

At 1st level, you gain proficiency in Arcana, Deception, or Intimidation (your choice). You also know Deep Speech.

Gravity Push

At 1st level, when you make a melee spell attack against a Medium or smaller creature, that creature is pushed back 10ft on a hit.

Channel Divinity: Glimpse of the Far Realm

Starting at the 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to project a mental image of the Far Realm on a creature.

As an action, you can choose one creature you can see within 60ft of you. That creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be stunned until the end of your next turn.

Channel Divinity: Tear to The Pocket

At 6th level, when a creature makes a melee attack roll against you, you can use your reaction to open a tear in reality with your Channel Divinity feature. If the creature’s attack roll is successful, it comes into contact with the tear and takes 3d6 psychic damage and you take half of their attack’s damage.

Alien Mind

Starting at 8th level, your thoughts cannot be read unless allowed by you. Additionally, you are able to read the surface thoughts of a creature you can see within 60ft.

Aura of Madness

At 17th level, as an action, you can emit an aura that pulses with alien energy. The aura is a sphere that is centered on you and has a 30ft radius. It lasts for 1 minute.

For the duration, any hostile creatures that start their turn in the sphere or enter it must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature becomes stunned by the alien figures and disembodied voices only it can perceive.

Once used, you can’t do so again until after a long rest.
